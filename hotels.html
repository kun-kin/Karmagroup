Karma Sunshine Village, Bangalore, India

Welcome to Karma Sunshine Village – A back-to-nature hideaway that’s a world away from the hustle and bustle of nearby Bangalore City. Bringing together a collection of rustic cabins with grass roofs, bamboo facades and cosy wooden interiors as well as unique little container homes, Karma Sunshine Village nestles in pretty landscaped gardens featuring a plunge pool, gazebo and fire pit – perfect for sunset-into-evening get togethers. Offering a host of on-site and off-site amenities and activities for families and friends, Karma Sunshine Village presents the perfect opportunity to relax and unwind for a few days with your nearest and dearest.

This property is part of Karma Group’s award winning Lifestyle and Leisure Club

Accommodation

Karma Sunshine Village comprises 12 comfy cabins that inspire a back to nature ambience with their native materials and peaceful garden surrounds. Each accommodation features a spacious double bed complete with mosquito net, en-suite bathrooms and windows looking out onto vistas of rolling countryside or the Nandi hills on the horizon.

Location

Karma Sitabani is located in Corbett, Nainital District of Uttrakhand. The magical landscape of Corbett is well known and fabled for its tiger richness. However it’s not only about TIGERS. Sure, it is called the Heaven of Majestic Tigers, but, it will not fall short to suffice ones eyes a view of the nature.

The Resort is a mesmerising retreat that transcends one to another world. Being situated close to the Corbett Tiger Reserve, home to several exotic and rare species of birds and animals, this is a perfect getaway for both; an adventure of a lifetime as well as blissful surrender to mother nature.

By road from Delhi to Karma Sitabani it would take approximately 6-7 hours. Flight from Delhi to Pantnagar would take an hour, Pant Nagar to Resort would be approx 2.30 Hrs.Train station(Ramnagar-26Kms / Haldwani -78 Kms ) is approximately takes 1-2 Hrs to the resort.
